THE KCOZM STORY

KCOZM, transforming the salon industry with luxurious salon experience. Asia’s Most Awarded Premium Salon Chain, KCOZM Salon is a one stop destination for premium hair, makeup, skin, nail & aesthetics services. Started in the year 2002 in the city beautiful Chandigarh with the main motive to provide top notch services to the clients of all walks of life. With over 23+ years of experience, 15 lakhs+ satisfied clients, 40+ successfully running salons & 2000+ well trained staff, KCOZM is providing world class salon experience.
